Know Your Recyclables

Powered by AI:


We are using a deep image recognition model to identify your items and provide you with personalized info to make recycling as quick and hassle-free as possible! We'll also share tips on ways to become more sustainable and circular-economy friendly.

Simply take a picture of an item and get all the info you need instantly! We will let you know:

  • If your item is recyclable
  • Where the nearest bin or collection point is (recycle on-the-go!)
  • If your item isn't recyclable, what alternative products are there on the market that will allow you to contribute to a more circular economy
